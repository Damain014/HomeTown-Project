Top Three Famous Place in Indore

Rajwada Palace

A historical edifice that once belonged to the Holkars of the Maratha Empire, Rajwada Palace is one of the oldest structures in Indore.

Patalpani Falls

It flows down in its full glory, leaving tourists mesmerized. Descending from a height of 300 feet, this roaring, and foamy waterfall.

Chappan Dukaan

Explore the flavors of Indore and be prepared to delight your taste .One of the most iconic food streets in the city. Hindi means “56”.
